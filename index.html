Steely Dan Band


American rock band founded by Walter Becker and Donald Fagen in 1972.


The Millennium Collection of Steely Dan


Steely Dan Timeline:

  • 1967 - Formative and early years (1967–1972)
  • 1972 - Can't Buy a Thrill and Countdown to Ecstasy (1972–1973)
  • 1974 - Pretzel Logic and Katy Lied (1974–1976)
  • 1976 - The Royal Scam and Aja (1976–1978)
  • 1978 - Gaucho and breakup (1978–1981)
  • 1981 - Time off (1981–1993)
  • 1993 - Reunion, Alive in America (1993–2000)
  • 2000 - Two Against Nature and Everything Must Go (2000–2003)
  • 2003 - Touring, solo activity (2003–2017)
  • 2017 - After Becker's death (2017–present)


The name Steely Dan is taken from the William Burroughs novel Naked Lunch. Combining funk, rock and jazz, Steely Dan music has been described as complex. Rolling Stone magazine called them: the perfect musical antiheroes for the seventies.
